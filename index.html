Pilot Application Website

   A productivity app that builds on the concept of Pomodoro Technique to help increase focus and let user stay organized. The app features a simple responsive design, interactive controls that allow the user to set a customizable Pomodoro timer, add personal tasks to the list of activities, play focus music during work blocks, and follow the built-in guided meditation activities during break times. The music and the rotating background images promote focus and productivity, and the toggle-able weather and news panels keep the user up-to-date on a need-to-know basis.
